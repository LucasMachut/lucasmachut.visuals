PERCEPTION

Before booking, the guest already feels something

Before choosing a hotel, the guest has already begun to imagine the experience.

They observe the light, the spaces, the rhythm, the details and the way everything appears connected. When that perception is clear, the image stops being just a presentation of the place and begins to take part in the decision.

WHAT I CREATE

  • Reels and social media content

  • Visual direction and narrative

    A reading of the place before production, to avoid generic or disconnected content.

THE GAZE

The gaze behind the images

I'm Lucas Machut, a French photographer and filmmaker based in Brazil.

Before working with images, my experience passed through performance: observing, understanding what doesn't immediately appear and translating an intention with precision.

This way of seeing directly shapes my work with hotels and experience venues. Before filming, I try to understand what the place communicates, what isn't yet clear and which images might make the guest feel something before arriving.

My gaze combines a European attention to sobriety, light and composition with a living relationship with the spaces, gestures and rhythms of Brazil.

It's not just about producing beautiful photos and videos. It's about visually translating what makes a place chosen.

REGARD

A visual reading before production

REGARD is my way of analysing how a hotel appears before producing new images.

From the website, Instagram, Booking and other channels, I observe what already works, what is unclear and which images could make the experience more coherent, desirable and easy to understand.

The goal is not to point out flaws in a generic way, but to identify where visual communication can gain clarity, continuity and strength.

This reading helps define a more precise direction for photos, videos, reels, website content or presentation materials.
